About Me

I am a self-taught front end web developer who started coding last year alongside my career as a performing arts teacher. Starting from my first “hello world” page, I developed an intrigue into how I could take this further and the journey began there. I have now created full applications, combining styling with functionality and leveraging open source frameworks to ensure best practice. Recently I have started learning React and using this library has given me a greater interest in front end development and creating interactive UI’s. I have discovered how fast this industry moves; which has encouraged me to continue learning, exploring, and adapting; something I look forward to as I start my career.
